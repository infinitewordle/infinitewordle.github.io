How To Play Letter Boxed

01

The Rule of Sides

Words must be formed by connecting letters on the sides of the box. You cannot use two letters from the same side consecutively.

02

The Chain

The **last letter** of one word must be the **first letter** of the next word in the chain.

03

The Goal

The objective is to use all 12 unique letters on the box sides in the fewest words possible (usually 2, 3, or 4).

04

Length is Key

Words must be at least 3 letters long.
