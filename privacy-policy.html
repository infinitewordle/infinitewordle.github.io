Privacy Policy

*Last Updated: December 10, 2025*

Your privacy is critically important to us. The core principle of **Infinite Wordle** is simplicity and accessibility, and that extends to our approach to your data. We aim to collect as little personal information as possible.

Information We Collect

1. Directly Collected Data: None.

We do not require accounts, sign-ups, or login credentials. We do not collect names, email addresses, payment information, or any other personally identifiable information (PII) directly from you to play the games.

2. Usage Data (Via Google Analytics):

We use **Google Analytics** to understand how the website is used and to improve the experience. This data is collected passively and is anonymized. It includes:

  • Pages visited and time spent on pages.
  • Device type (desktop, mobile) and operating system.
  • Referring website.

This data is not linked back to individual users. You can opt-out of Google Analytics tracking by installing Google's opt-out browser add-on.

3. Third-Party Game Content:

Our games are hosted via iframes embedding third-party content (e.g., from *wordle-unblocked.github.io*). While we do not control the data collection practices of these external sites, they typically use **local browser storage (cookies)** to save your game progress (like win streaks and statistics). We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of the third-party providers for full details.

Data Storage and Security

All information collected via Google Analytics is stored securely on Google's servers. Since we do not collect any PII, there is no personal user data stored on our servers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
